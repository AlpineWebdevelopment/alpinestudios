ABOUT US

We are Alpine Studios, a young web development duo dedicated to crafting exceptional digital experiences. With a passion for innovation and a commitment to quality, we blend stunning design with powerful functionality to bring websites to life.

As the Frontend Designer & Client Manager, Balazs Varga creates designs that convert visitors to customers and makes brands trustworthy. Mainly focuses on creating visually engaging, user-friendly interfaces while ensuring seamless communication and project execution. On the development side, Simon Severin (a.k.a. @severinnHQ), our expert Web Developer, turns ideas into high-performing, scalable websites with clean & efficient code and outstanding SEO.

Together, we specialize in building modern, responsive, and high-performing websites tailored to our clients' needs. Whether you're a startup, a growing business, or an established brand, we're here to elevate your online presence with creativity and expertise.
